About HeartCare

HeartCare is a heart disease risk prediction system built to assist individuals in understanding their cardiovascular health and taking preventive action at the right time. This system is created for education, awareness, and early risk identification.

Heart Risk Analysis

We assess multiple health and lifestyle parameters to identify possible heart disease risk levels.

Accurate Prediction

Our system processes medical and lifestyle information to provide reliable statistical predictions.

Data Privacy

Your health data is kept private and secure. We never store user data permanently.

Medical Awareness

This platform supports early detection and promotes regular health checkups and awareness.

Our Mission

To provide an accessible and reliable heart disease risk prediction system that helps individuals understand their heart health and take preventive actions early.

Our Vision

To contribute towards reducing heart disease cases by encouraging regular health monitoring, awareness, and early preventive care.
